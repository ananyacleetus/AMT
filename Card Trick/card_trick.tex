1.)
\\
\begin{proof}
 Let $a \in \mathbb{Z}$ where $a$ = 2$k$ +1. Therefore,
  \begin{align*}
    a^2 + 3a + 5 &= 2(k+1)^2 + 3(2k+1) + 5 \\
    &= 4k^2 + 4k +1 + 6k + 3 +5 \\
    &= 4k^2 + 10k + 9 \\
    &= 4k^2 + 10k + 8 + 1 \\
    &= 2(2k^2 + 5k + 4) +1 \\
  \end{align*}
  By the definition of odd, $a^2+3a+5$ is odd \\
 
\end{proof}
